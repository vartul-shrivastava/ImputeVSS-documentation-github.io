Pipeline Management
===================

What is a Pipeline?
-------------------

A pipeline is a sequence of imputation steps applied to a dataset. It ensures consistency and repeatability across data-cleaning workflows.

Managing Pipelines
------------------

1. **Saving a Pipeline:**
   - After applying imputation methods, click **Export Pipeline** to save the steps as a JSON file.

2. **Loading a Pipeline:**
   - Use the **Import Pipeline** button to load a previously saved pipeline.

3. **Applying Pipelines:**
   - After loading a pipeline, click **Apply Pipeline** to execute the steps sequentially.
