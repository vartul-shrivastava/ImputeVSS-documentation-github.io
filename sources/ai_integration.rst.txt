AI Integration
==============

ImputeVSS integrates with Ollama to provide AI-based recommendations for data imputation.

Steps for AI Summaries
----------------------

1. **Set Up AI:**
   - Ensure Ollama is installed and running.

2. **Customize the Prompt (Optional):**
   - Edit the AI prompt via the **Customize Prompt** button.
   - Include `{table}` in the prompt to provide context from the statistics table.

3. **Select an AI Model:**
   - Choose a model from the dropdown menu.

4. **Generate a Summary:**
   - Click **Get AI Recommendation** to generate an AI-based summary.
